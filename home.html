Brendon Urie

Lead singer of Panic! At The Disco

Brendon Boyd Urie (born April 12, 1987) is an American singer, songwriter, and musician, best known as the lead vocalist and multi-instrumentalist of Panic! at the Disco, of which he is the only original member remaining.
